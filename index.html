Everything You Need

A Full Studio. In Your Browser.

Every tool a real producer uses — designed so anyone can make music from day one.

🤖

AI Track Starter

Describe your vibe in plain English. Groq generates a chord progression, bassline pattern, suggested scale, and a creative direction — your blank canvas becomes a real song in seconds.

Chord Progressions Bassline Patterns Scale Selection Creative Direction
🎛️

Step Sequencer

16-step grid with color-coded rows for drums, bass, and melody. Add rows, mute channels, and watch the glowing playhead sweep in perfect time.

🎹

Playable Keyboard

Two octaves, mouse or QWERTY keys. In-scale notes glow so you always know which keys sound good — beginners play like pros instantly.

🥁

Drum Machine

4×4 velocity-sensitive pads. Choose from Acoustic, Electronic, Lo-Fi, or Trap kits. Every pad mapped to keyboard shortcuts for fast performance.

🎼

Chord Pads

Eight pads pre-loaded from your AI progression. Tap to play full chords — no music theory needed. Ripple animations on every hit.

✨

Audio-Reactive Visualizer

Three stunning modes that respond in real time — Particle Field with physics simulation, Waveform oscillation, and Frequency Bars. Colors shift with your mood setting. 60fps. Zero lag.

🎚️

Mixer

Professional channel strips with volume faders, pan, mute/solo, and live level meters that bounce with every beat.

🔊

Effects Rack

Reverb, BPM-synced delay, and distortion. Toggle any effect instantly — add warmth, space, or grit to the master output.

🎵

AI Melody Generator

Click once and Groq writes a melodic phrase in your current scale and mood. Keep it, tweak it, or regenerate until it's perfect.

⬇️

Record & Export

Capture everything playing through the Web Audio API. Export your track as a WAV file named automatically by mood and BPM.
