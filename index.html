16-Step Sequencer

Color-coded rows for kick, snare, hi-hat, bass, lead, and melody. Click to toggle steps, drag to paint patterns, mute rows instantly. A glowing playhead sweeps in perfect time.

  • Swing timing control
  • Per-row volume faders
  • Piano Roll for each melodic row
  • Add unlimited rows
  • Randomize patterns
🎹

Playable Keyboard

Two octaves you can play with mouse or keyboard shortcuts. In-scale notes glow automatically — beginners sound musical immediately, pros can lay down melodies fast.

  • 6 scales: Major, Minor, Pentatonic, Blues, Dorian, Mixolydian
  • Octave up/down (Z/X keys)
  • Touch / mobile support
  • Record-to-sequencer mode
🥁

Drum Machine

16 velocity-sensitive pads in a 4×4 grid. Every pad has a keyboard shortcut for live performance. Switch between four drum kits: Acoustic, Electronic, Lo-Fi, and Trap.

  • 4 drum kits
  • Keyboard-mapped shortcuts
  • Web Audio synthesis
  • Hit animation + flash
🎼

Chord Pads

Eight pads pre-loaded with your AI chord progression. One click plays a full chord. Ripple animations on every hit. Connect the Arpeggiator to auto-arpeggiate any chord.

  • AI-loaded progressions
  • Ripple touch animation
  • Arpeggiator integration
  • Up / Down / Random modes
✨

Audio-Reactive Visualizer

Three full-canvas modes that react to everything playing through the audio engine in real time. Colors shift with your mood. Runs at 60fps using the Canvas API and Web Audio Analyser node — zero performance cost.

Particle Field
Waveform
Frequency Bars
🎚️

Mixer + Live Meters

A channel strip for every sequencer row with volume faders, mute/solo buttons, and level meters that bounce in time with each beat. Master volume on the transport bar.

  • Per-channel volume
  • Mute and Solo
  • Live VU meters
  • Auto-synced from sequencer
🔊

Effects Rack

Four professional-grade effects on the master output, each with a toggle switch and continuous amount slider. All implemented with Web Audio API nodes.

  • Reverb (Convolver)
  • BPM-synced Delay
  • Distortion (WaveShaper)
  • Dynamics Compressor
🎵

Piano Roll Editor

Open a full piano roll for any melodic row in the sequencer. Click cells to place notes across all octaves, clear, or randomize. Your edits sync back instantly.

  • Per-row note editing
  • 36 pitch rows (3 octaves)
  • Click to toggle notes
  • Randomize button
🌀

Arpeggiator

Set the mode and rate, hold a chord pad — the arpeggiator plays individual notes of the chord in sequence, synced perfectly to your BPM.

  • Up / Down / Up-Down / Random
  • 1/4, 1/8, 1/16 note rates
  • BPM-synced timing
  • Works with Chord Pads
⬇️

Record & Export

Capture the full mixed output of the Web Audio engine. Hit record, play your track, stop — and download a real audio file named automatically by mood and BPM.

  • MediaRecorder API
  • All effects included in export
  • Auto-named by mood + BPM
  • No upload or processing
💾

Project Save & Load

Save up to 10 projects to your browser's localStorage. Each project stores the full sequencer pattern, BPM, and row configuration. Load any project in one click.

  • Up to 10 saved projects
  • Pattern + BPM + rows
  • No account needed
  • Share link via clipboard
